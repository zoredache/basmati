You can "link" your assignments to Washington State standards by using the Miscellaneous field within the Assignment table of Grade Machine. For example, suppose you just finished grading an assignment that relates to the Math 1.3 standard (understand and apply concepts and procedures from geometric sense ) and Science 2.2 (apply science knowledge and skills to solve problems or meet challenges). In the Miscellanous column of Grade Machine, enter Math 1.3, Science 2.2. This will trigger Basmati to "link" these assignments to the state standards -- providing parents and students with more information about your assignment.

In the table below, you'll find the abbreviations to all of the standards. To correctly enter these into Grade Machine, simply type the abbreviation, a space, and then a number corresponding to the specific strand. If your assignment meets more than one standard, separate the list with commas.

You must also make certain that the phrase "standard:wa" is included in the MISC 3 field in the Class Information menu.
