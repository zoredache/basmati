Obtaining Student Passwords

Once you've submitted grades to Basmati, you can obtain passwords so that students can access their own grades. Follow the steps below...
  1. Log into the faculty section of Basmati using your username and password.
  2. Click the "Show Current Classes" link.
  3. A list of your classes should appear (including the course-codes and the date your grades were last updated).
  4. Click the button to obtain a list of passwords for a particular class.
  5. The next page will show a list of student IDs and passwords -- feel free to print this page, cut it up and hand them out to students. You can also check a student's grade by clicking on the button.
